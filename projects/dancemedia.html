Dancemedia

Dance Magazine is an influential American trade publication for dance published by the Macfadden Communications Group. It was first published in June 1927 as The American Dancer. Dance Magazine has multiple sister publications, including Pointe, Dance Spirit, Dance Teacher, Dance 212, and DanceU101. Dance Magazine.

Moodboard

Dance is all about movement, curve or sharp, fast or slow rhythms, repetition, choreographies... We wanted some elegance and simplicity for the website, and because dance is about body and the essenciality of its movement, the colors are just white, black and the orange of the brand. Orange appeared like the body in movement, that, as the fire, leaves its light trail on the space. The website is full of geometric forms and curves that stablish this connection with movement and freedom..

Colours

The palette is made up of patterns, dark greys and whites. These patterns work well as a background with the dark grey or white version of the logo, or as the interior colour of the small ampersand when the logo appears on white surfaces.

Styles & Typography

Dancemedia needed a typeface that was fresh, a bit elegant and with good redability for big titles and body text. Helvetica us a universal stylish typography that allows a lot of options for bif titles and small text.
