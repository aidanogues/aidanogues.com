Dancemedia

Dance Magazine is an influential American trade publication for dance Dance Magazine has multiple sister publications, including Pointe, Dance Spirit, Dance Teacher, Dance 212, and DanceU101. Website's design were outdated and they searched for optimal ways to keep readers attention to the articles to improve as well advertisemnt on their publications.

Moodboard

Dance is all about movement, curve or sharp, fast or slow rhythms, repetition, choreographies... We wanted some elegance and simplicity for the website, and because dance is about body and the essenciality of its movements, the colors are just white, black and the orange of the brand. Orange appeared like the body in movement, that, as the fire, leaves its light trail on the space. The website is full of geometric forms and curves that stablish this connection with movement and freedom..

Colours

The palette is made of its essencial colors: black, white and orange. While orange is the main brand's color, white works as an airy background, fluid and fresh, and black as a high contrast texts.

Styles & Typography

Dancemedia needed a typeface that was fresh, a bit elegant and with good redability for big titles and body text. Helvetica us a universal stylish typography that allows a lot of options for big titles and small text. Almost all the website text is built with a light weight, allowing bigger texts and adding this extra light texture and elegance.

Homepage Layout

Content layouts were used to incorporate all the different elements and features we wanted on the different pages. These content blocks allowed us to identify what content was important to users on each page. This is what the layouts above looked like without images added. A very important element was the infinite scroll model, where we allow users to keep scrolling and reading the content in the same page, without jumping between pages.

Final Pages

This is the result of the different pages I designed (homepage, aticle page, category page and navbar's dropdowns. The structure should be very simple and flexible to add different modules such as newsletter's modules, paper magazine, social media, quizzes, events, etc.
